Hello, My Name is Kelsie.

  • Graphic Designer
  • Photographer
    •

I've been designing since I was a child. Art has been my passion and creating is what motivates me in life. Since the time I was two, I found every reason to draw and as I got older I applied my talent to every day things. I've wanted to pursue college since I was young but life had other plans for me. I settled down and had two children and married. Once my youngest was one, I applied to an online program through F.I.R.S.T Institute.

Once at F.I.R.S.T Institute I took a course that explored me into photography. I found that I love doing candid photos and environmental photos. Capturing the moment of people in their own space and feelings is moving to me. Doing photography is newer to me than designing is. I love it just as much.
